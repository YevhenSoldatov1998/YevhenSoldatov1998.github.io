Multi-cryptocurrency wallet NextON

Multifunctional wallet NextON is an innovative business tool for entrepreneurs to start a revolution in the field of e-commerce.

The wallet solves several problems for business representatives at once:

  • Allows to bypass the bureaucratic procedures and accept payment for goods in crypto-currencies without charging commissions;
  • Maintains statistics and accounting of your business in the form of graphs, charts, tables.

In the future, the team plans to implement the following steps to achieve the mission:

  • Release wallets for Windows, Linux, Mac, Web, Android and IOS;
  • Improve the interface, user experience, and functionality of the wallet;
  • Release hardware wallets, such as Ledger and Trezor;
  • Develop widgets for CMS such as Wordpress, Drupal, Joomla, Opencart, Magento;
  • Conclude a partnership with online shops.
